McKinsey Client Capabilities Network

Delivering sustainable and inclusive growth through our proprietary knowledge and capabilities, deep expertise, and distinctive insights

Our ~8,000 client capabilities professionals across the globe work alongside our consultants to deliver sustainable and inclusive growth. Further, they are core to our firm’s R&D, developing new knowledge and building and refining proprietary capabilities (e.g., assets, data sets), as well as curating, managing, and maintaining them. Our portfolio includes state-of-the-art research, asset-backed and rapid diagnostics, benchmarking, and workflow support. Our professionals have deep expertise and are able to apply a broad range of methodologies for specialized on-demand analyses.
